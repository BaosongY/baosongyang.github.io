Biography

I am a postdoctoral research fellow working with Philipp Koehn on statistical machine translation. I earned my Ph.D. in computer science at the University of Maryland, where I worked with Philip Resnik. Prior to graduate school, I was a software engineer at IBM, and prior to that I studied computer science at Duke University. I did not work with anyone named Philip(p) at IBM or Duke. See my University homepage for my academic history.
